# Missing Input Response: Discovery

Use this response when the discovery brief is incomplete.

Ask for the missing items from this list:

- Goal: What is the main business or user goal?
- Features: What must the page include or support?
- Audience: Who is the exact target visitor?
- Business context: What product, offer, brand, or organization is this for?
- Scope: What is included and excluded?
- Required sections: Which sections must appear?
- Content facts: What facts, claims, pricing, testimonials, or proof are approved?
- Brand/design: Any colors, style, tone, references, or avoid list?
- Technical constraints: Existing stack, hosting, CMS, forms, analytics, integrations?
- SEO/accessibility/compliance: Any requirements?
- Deployment: Where should it go live?

Do not create a full discovery summary until blockers are answered.
