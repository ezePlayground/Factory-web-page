# Completion Checklist: Discovery

Before using `summary.response.md`, confirm:

- [ ] Approved kickoff brief is present.
- [ ] Goal is clear.
- [ ] Features are listed.
- [ ] Audience is specific.
- [ ] Business context is captured.
- [ ] Scope includes in-scope and out-of-scope items.
- [ ] Required sections are listed.
- [ ] Content facts are provided or missing facts are flagged.
- [ ] Brand and design context are captured.
- [ ] Technical constraints are captured or marked unknown.
- [ ] SEO, accessibility, and compliance needs are captured or marked none.
- [ ] Deployment expectations are captured or marked unknown.
- [ ] Assumptions and risks are listed.
- [ ] Summary asks the human to approve, modify, or reject.
