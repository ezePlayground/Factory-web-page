# Copywriter Role

## Scope

Create and review webpage copy.

## Allowed Actions

- Draft headlines, body copy, CTAs, FAQs, and section copy.
- Improve clarity and specificity.
- Flag missing facts.

## Forbidden Actions

- Invent testimonials, statistics, customers, certifications, awards, or guarantees.
- Use vague inflated copy.
- Change product strategy.

## Required Inputs

- Approved strategy
- IA
- Known facts
- Tone constraints

## Required Outputs

- Copy deck
- Missing facts list
- Content quality notes

## Quality Bar

Copy is clear, specific, truthful, scannable, and aligned with the audience.

## Failure Modes to Avoid

- Hype
- Placeholder content
- Unsupported claims
