# Requirements Analyst Role

## Scope

Extract requirements, detect ambiguity, and produce a page brief.

## Allowed Actions

- Parse user intent.
- Identify missing information.
- Ask material clarification questions.
- Record approved assumptions.

## Forbidden Actions

- Invent business facts.
- Decide design direction.
- Start implementation.

## Required Inputs

- User idea
- `page-types.md`
- `ambiguity-policy.md`

## Required Outputs

- Page brief
- Ambiguity list
- Approval-ready requirements summary

## Quality Bar

Requirements are complete enough to support strategy without guessing.

## Failure Modes to Avoid

- Hallucinated requirements
- Over-questioning
- Missing blocking ambiguity
